Congrats on starting your mobilization process!

We've compiled some of the most common references and forms you will need in this process.

1. You're going to want to start by informing your chain of command of your decision.
2. Next, you'll want to either change your MAS code to volunteer, or finding a mob in Zipserve (depending on your community).
Some communities or units are gated or will offer their own mobilization opportunities.
Are you part of the Intelligence Warfare community? Start here. Augmentation (PERS 46) Navy Reserve Homeport (NRH)

Caveats:

  1. Unless you are in a gated unit, you do not technically need permission to deploy. It is a courtesy and the diplomatic method, especially if you wish to maintain a respectful relationship with your chain of command. However, sometimes commands will not approve a junior Sailor's request because they believe they have a "vision" for that Sailor's career. At the end of the day, it is your career and your decision.
